## Example usage

Use the examples to show different relationship-intelligence patterns:

1. **Mayberg Foundation**: map trustees, rising trustees, executive leadership, grantees, and event relationships.
2. **ACSZ**: map hospital mission, regional fundraising leadership, major-donor ecosystems, foundations, and community connectors.
3. **Teach Coalition**: map donors, school networks, policymakers, community coalitions, and regional leadership.
4. **Camp Jori**: map board members, families, alumni, staff, seasonal-talent pipelines, community partners, and vendors.
5. **Oak Valley Hospital District**: map board members, interim leadership, executive succession, departmental leadership gaps, operating priorities, and follow-on search opportunities.

Do not expose personal contact data. Use only the public organizational and professional context needed for the demonstration.
